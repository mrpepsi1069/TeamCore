What it does

BUILT FOR
TEAMS

Everything your team Discord needs — from scheduling to stats — without the bloat.

⏰
Game Scheduling
Create game time polls with multiple time slots. Players click buttons to mark availability — results update in real time.
📋
Lineup Management
Build, edit, and post team lineups. Track positions, add or remove players, and post formatted lineups to any channel.
📊
Depth Charts
Maintain full depth charts with promote and demote controls. Keep every position stacked and organized.
📜
Contracts
Add and manage player contracts with mark-paid and delete buttons. Post all active contracts in a clean embed.
🏅
Awards & Rings
Hand out custom awards and championship rings. Players check their own with a single command.
👑
Team Management
Create leagues with signup buttons that auto-assign Discord roles. Players join or leave by clicking.
🔔
Team Broadcasts
DM every member of a role at once with a custom message. Keep your whole roster in the loop instantly.
🛡️
Moderation
Kick, ban, timeout, mute voice channels. Full hierarchy checks so managers can't moderate above their rank.
⚙️
Server Setup
Configure channels and roles for each permission tier. Adapts to your server's existing structure.
